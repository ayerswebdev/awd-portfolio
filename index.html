About Me

I am a front-end web developer currently working as a high school math teacher in Warsaw, Illinois. I'm in the process of getting job-ready so that I can make the jump into computer science as a career rather than a side job (and move to Vegas). When I'm not preparing lessons or building websites, you're most likely to find me at the golf course or the casino!


I have experience in the front-end using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript (vanilla or jQuery). I am also proficient in the use of CSS preprocessors such as SASS and front-end frameworks such as Bootstrap. I'm a fast learner and I love to learn new things, so if your project requires me to learn something else I'm all for it!

Basic Chess Timer

At my first teaching job, I was the teacher in charge of the school's chess club. There was a 25 minute time during each day during which the club met, but we found that many games weren't getting done in that time because people were playing too slowly. So I built this timer and had students use it to ensure that the pace of play was quick. In this application, the two players agree to a time per turn, then set it and switch turns with a button press. Easy and effective!
